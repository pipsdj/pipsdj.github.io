Areas of Development

Below are the major areas in which we focus a traders development into being a consistently profitable trader.

Forex trading is a powerful form of personal empowerment and if done correctly leads to financial freedom. It is much too easy for a trader to be able to make good trading decisions but not become financially free, this easily happens when a trader is able to effectively follow instruction and make disciplined trading decisions. Unfortunately trading decisions and financial decisions diverge as a trader gains success in the markets.

You might be thinking to yourself, But the trader is making profits? profits come and go, which is why the trader is not financially free, needing to constantly open and close positions on a daily basis in order to sustain the newly acquired lavish lifestyle.

Financial education is a valuable tool in being able to make good investments in the markets, take note of the thin line between an investor and a trader. An investor takes less risk and foresees a larger profit while ignoring short term price movements. A trader contrary to that is constantly exposed to risk trying to profit from small price fluctuations, there is no real sustainability in overexposure to risk, in addition other factors that affect a traders personal performance. listed below are the major focus areas of trader education.
